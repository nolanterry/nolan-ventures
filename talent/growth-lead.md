# Growth Lead — Nolan Ventures

## The Role

Drive distribution and user acquisition across a portfolio of 4 vertical SaaS products. You'll own the full top-of-funnel: getting the right people to find, try, and pay for our products. Heavy emphasis on organic channels and scrappy distribution — this isn't a "run Facebook ads" role.

## What You'll Do

- **SEO & Content Distribution** — optimize 120+ existing blog pages, build backlinks, get pages ranking. Content is written; you make it discoverable.
- **Community & Directory Presence** — get products listed on G2, Capterra, Product Hunt, industry directories, trade association sites, Reddit communities
- **Cold Outbound** — set up and manage Instantly campaigns targeting verified buyer lists (sequences are drafted, need execution)
- **Partnerships** — identify integration partners, co-marketing opportunities, and referral channels in each vertical
- **Conversion Optimization** — A/B test landing pages, CTAs, pricing pages. Improve signup → paid conversion
- **Analytics** — own PostHog/GA dashboards, track CAC, activation rates, channel attribution
- **Launch Playbooks** — develop repeatable launch process for new products (we ship fast, need distribution to match)

## Portfolio You'll Work Across

| Product | Vertical | Stage | Primary Channel |
|---------|----------|-------|----------------|
| FenceCalc | Fence contractors | Later stage, deployed | SEO + outbound |
| FireLog | Fire safety / inspections | Early | SEO + directories |
| CraneCheck | Crane operators / OSHA | Early | Trade associations + outbound |
| Scian platform | B2B SaaS GTM | Building | LinkedIn + partnerships |

## Required Skills

- 2+ years in growth, demand gen, or distribution (SaaS preferred)
- Proven SEO results — you've ranked pages, not just "done keyword research"
- Experience running cold outbound at scale (Instantly, Smartlead, or similar)
- Can set up and interpret analytics (PostHog, GA4, Search Console)
- Comfortable working across multiple products simultaneously
- Strong copywriting — landing pages, emails, ad copy
- Self-starter who operates on outcomes, not tasks

## Nice to Have

- Experience in vertical SaaS or niche B2B markets
- Product Hunt launch experience
- Backlink building / digital PR
- Community management (Reddit, niche forums, trade groups)
- Familiarity with Clay for lead enrichment
- Basic HTML/CSS for landing page tweaks

## Hours & Structure

- Overlap with ET business hours preferred
- Ramp to full-time as products gain traction

## Success Metrics (First 90 Days)

- **Month 1:** Google Search Console verified on all sites, all products listed on 5+ directories, first outbound campaign live
- **Month 2:** Organic traffic up 50%+ from baseline, 3+ Product Hunt/community launches, outbound generating demo requests
- **Month 3:** First paid customers attributed to their channels, repeatable launch playbook documented, CAC benchmarks established

## Sequencing Note

**Hire the Product Engineer first.** Growth efforts are wasted if the product breaks when users arrive. Get the apps stable, tested, and monitored — then pour traffic on them. Ideal start date for Growth Lead: 30 days after Product Engineer is onboarded.
